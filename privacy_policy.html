Privacy Policy

GMassMailer - Email Campaign Manager

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how GMassMailer ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Gmail extension for email campaign management.

Effective Date: This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date you first use our extension and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Gmail Account Information: We may access your Gmail account to send emails on your behalf.
  • Campaign Data: Email content, recipient lists, and settings you create.
  • Contact Information: Emails and names from your CSV uploads.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Feature usage and frequency.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, version, and extension info.
  • Error Logs: Technical error details to help diagnose issues.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain email campaign functionality
  • To send emails using your Gmail account
  • To manage your campaigns, templates, and recipients
  • To improve extension features and experience
  • To respond to support requests
  • To protect against fraud or misuse

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most data is stored locally using Chrome's storage APIs, including:

  • Campaigns and templates
  • Recipient lists
  • User settings

4.2 Data Security

  • Encrypted communication over HTTPS
  • Data protected by Chrome's security model
  • No Gmail credentials or tokens stored on our servers
  • Permissions limited to your consent

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Providers: Limited data shared to operate services
  • Legal: Shared if legally required
  • Business Transfers: In case of acquisition or restructuring

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: View your data via extension interface
  • Deletion: Uninstall or clear data to delete
  • Revoke Access: Through your Google account settings
  • Export: Export campaign and contact data

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google APIs: For sending emails and account access
  • Google OAuth 2.0: For secure login/authentication

We encourage reviewing their respective privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data. Please contact us if a minor has submitted information.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • Updated on this page
  • "Last Updated" date will reflect changes
  • Major changes notified within the extension UI

Continued use indicates acceptance of updates.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your region. We follow applicable laws and use safeguards to protect it.
